Fund projects that increase human lifespan

LifeFund is crowdsourcing platform that connects together people who want to do as much as they can to prolong the human lifespan through science and engineering.

You can propose projects, rank projects, and fund projects. You can even pay people to propose or to rank projects.

The hope is that a community of people collaborating in a structured manner will accelerate the development of technologies to defeat diseases and allow healthy aging.

This project is run is by Umar Nawaz, a computer programmer based out of Toronto, Canada.
